The Technical Execution

This is how the physical journey and the math come together in optimized SQL.

SQL
SELECT 
    c.CompanyName, 
    -- THE MATH: (Price * Qty)
    -- THE FORMATTING: COALESCE turns "Missing Payment" into 0
    COALESCE(SUM(od.UnitPrice * od.Quantity), 0) AS TotalSpending
FROM Customers c
-- THE VISIBILITY: LEFT JOIN ensures customers with NO orders still appear
LEFT JOIN Orders o ON c.CustomerID = o.CustomerID
LEFT JOIN "Order Details" od ON o.OrderID = od.OrderID
GROUP BY c.CompanyName
ORDER BY TotalSpending DESC;

The Concept

Total Spending = Revenue = Product Sales. The math remains Price * Qty regardless of who asks.

The Join Rule

No purchase = No record in the Orders table. LEFT JOIN is mandatory to keep them in view.

The Toolset

JOIN handles who shows up at the door; COALESCE handles how their empty hands are displayed.
